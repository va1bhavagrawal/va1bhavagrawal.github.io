Compass Control: Multi-Object Orientation Control for Text-to-Image Generation
Vaibhav Agrawal*, Rishubh Parihar*, Sachidanand VS, Venkatesh Babu Radhakrishnan,
CVPR, 2025
arXiv

A method for multi-object orientation control in T2I diffusion models. We show that an encoder can map a 3D orientation for an object to a special token, and the attention maps of these tokens can be constrained spatially to enable disentangled multi-object control.
