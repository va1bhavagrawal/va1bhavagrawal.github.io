Vaibhav Agrawal is an undergraduate researcher at the Center for Visual Information Technology (CVIT), IIIT-Hyderabad. He is jointly advised by Ravi Kiran S and Venkatesh Babu Radhakrishnan from the Vision and AI Lab (VAL) at IISc Bangalore. His research focuses on computer vision and deep learning. He has co-authored papers accepted at prestigious conferences, including CVPR 2025 and ICPR 2024.
